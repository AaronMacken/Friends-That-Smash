Friends That Smash

How it Works

Create Your Own League

Friends That Smash allows you to create your own personal Super Smash Bros. league. Use the built in tools to manage a league for your friends, co-workers, or local members of your community!

Recruit Friends

Use the Smash Finder to find people to smash with! Find other leagues to merge with or challenge for an all out battle royal!

Organize a Tournament

Friends That Smash lets you organize your own tournaments. Automatically generate a roster & schedule matches or manually create the tournament yourself. Get together and prove your worth!
